# 版块 2 概率应用 (仅数一、数三)

## A. 实用解题工具

### 第 1 组: 大数定律

切比雪夫: $\left\{  \begin{array}{lll} \text{ 设 }{X}_{1},{X}_{2}\cdots ,{X}_{n},\cdots \text{ 为两两不相关的随机变量序列,存在常数 }C \\  \text{ 使得 }D\left( {X}_{i}\right)  \leq  C\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots }\right) ,\text{ 则对于任意正数 }\varepsilon , \\  \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}P\left\{  {\left| {\frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{X}_{i} - \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}E\left( {X}_{i}\right) }\right|  < \varepsilon }\right\}   = 1 \end{array}\right.$

伯努利:   $\left\{  \begin{array}{l}\text{ 设 }{X}_{1},{X}_{2}\cdots ,{X}_{n},\cdots \text{ 相互独立,同服从 }0 - 1\text{ 分布,则对任意正整数 }\varepsilon\text{，有} \\  \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}P\left\{  {\left| {\frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{X}_{i} - p}\right|  < \varepsilon }\right\}   = 1 \end{array}\right.$ 

辛钦: $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{ 设 }{X}_{1},{X}_{2}\cdots ,{X}_{n},\cdots \text{相互独立同分布且期望为}\mu \text{，则对任意正整数 }\varepsilon\text{，有} \\  \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}P\left\{  {\left| {\frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{X}_{i} - \mu }\right|  < \varepsilon }\right\}   = 1 \end{array}\right.$

### 第 2 组: 依概率收敛

依概率收敛: 若 ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\ldots$ ,是一个随机变量序列, $A$ 是一个常数,若对于任意的 $\varepsilon  > 0$ ,

$$
\text{有}\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}P\left\{  {\left| {{X}_{n} - A}\right|  < \varepsilon }\right\}   = 1
$$

则称随机变量序列 ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\ldots$ 依概率收敛于常数 $A$ ,记作 ${X}_{n}\xrightarrow[]{P}A$

### 第 3 组: 分位数

定义: 设 $X$ ( $X$ 是总体或随机变量)的概率密度为 $f\left( x\right) ,F\left( x\right)$ 为其分布函数,对给定的实数 $\alpha  \in  \left( {0,1}\right)$ ,使  $P\left\{  {X \leq  {v}_{\alpha }}\right\}   = 1 - \alpha$ 的数 ${v}_{\alpha }$ ,称为分布函数 $F$ 的上侧分位数,即 $P\left\{  {X > {v}_{\alpha }}\right\}   = {\int }_{{v}_{\alpha }}^{+\infty }f\left( x\right) {dx} = \alpha$ .

示例: 对于 $N\left( {0,1}\right)$ ,满足 $\Phi \left( {u}_{\alpha }\right)  = 1 - \alpha$ 的数 ${u}_{\alpha }$ ,称为 $N\left( {0,1}\right)$ 的上侧 $\alpha$ 分位数(这里更 $\Phi$ 为标准正态分布 $N\left( {0,1}\right)$ 的分布函数).

## B. 核心问题及解题方法总结

 **1. 利用切比雪夫不等式计算

方法: 切比雪夫不等式: $P\{ \left| {X - {EX}}\right|  \geq  \varepsilon \}  \leq  \frac{DX}{{\varepsilon }^{2}}$ 或 $P\{ \left| {X - {EX}}\right|  < \varepsilon \}  \geq  1 - \frac{DX}{{\varepsilon }^{2}}$

例题 1. 在区间 $\left\lbrack  {1,2}\right\rbrack$ 内随机取 50 个数 ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\ldots ,{X}_{50}$ ,则由切比雪夫不等式可得概率  $P\left\{  {{70} < \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{{50}}{X}_{i} < {80}}\right\}   \geq$ \_\_\_\_\_.

【解】记 $X = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{{50}}{X}_{i}$ . 由题设条件可知 ${X}_{i}\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots ,{50}}\right)$ 服从区间 $\left\lbrack  {1,2}\right\rbrack$ 上的均匀分布,且  ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\ldots ,{X}_{50}$ 相互独立,

于是 $E\left( {X}_{i}\right)  = \frac{3}{2},D\left( {X}_{i}\right)  = \frac{1}{12},i = 1,2,\cdots ,{50}.,E\left( X\right)  = E\left( {\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{{50}}{X}_{i}}\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{{50}}E\left( {X}_{i}\right)  = \frac{3}{2} \times  {50} = {75}$ ,

$D\left( X\right)  = D\left( {\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{{50}}{X}_{i}}\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{{50}}D\left( {X}_{i}\right)  = \frac{1}{12} \times  {50} = \frac{25}{6}.$

根据切比雪夫不等式得:

$$
P\left\{  {{70} < \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{{50}}{X}_{i} < {80}}\right\}   = P\{ {70} < X < {80}\}  = P\{ {70} < E\left( X\right)  < X - E\left( X\right)  < {80} - E\left( X\right) \}
$$

$$
= P\{  - 5 < X - E\left( X\right)  < 5\}  = P\{ X - E\left( X\right)  < 5\}  \geq  1 - \frac{D\left( X\right) }{{5}^{2}} = 1 - \frac{1}{25} \cdot  \frac{25}{6} = \frac{5}{6}\text{.}
$$

 **2.利用中心极限定理计算**

棣莫-弗拉普斯定理: 
设 ${X}_{i} \sim  B\left( {n,p}\right)$ ,则对于任意实数 $x$ , $\text{有}\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}P\left\{  {\frac{\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{X}_{i} - {np}}{\sqrt{{np}\left( {1 - p}\right) }} \leq  x}\right\}   = {\int }_{-\infty }^{x}\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi }}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{t}^{2}}{2}}{dt}$

列维 - 林德伯格定理: 
设 ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\ldots$ ,相互独立且同分布,且期望 $\mu$ 方差 ${\sigma }^{2}$ 存在,则对于任意实数 $x$ ,有 $\mathop{\lim }\limits_ {{n \rightarrow  \infty }} P\left\{  {\frac{\mathop{\sum }\limits_ {{i = 1}} ^{n}{X}_{i} - {n\mu }}{\sqrt{n}\sigma } \leq  x}\right\}   = {\int }_{-\infty }^{x}\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi }}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac {{t}^{2}} {2}}{dt}$


 **3.三大抽样分布计算

抽样分布: $\left\{  \begin{array}{lllll} \text{ 条件 : }X \sim  {X}_{1},X \sim  {X}_{n}\text{,相互独立,且同服从 }N\left( {0,1}\right) \\  \text{ 结论 : }{\chi }^{2} = {X}_{1}^{2} + {X}_{2}^{2} + \cdots  + {X}_{n}^{2} \sim  {\chi }^{2}\left( n\right) \\  \text{ 结论 : }T = \frac{X}{\sqrt{Y/n}} \sim  t\left( n\right) \\  \text{ 结论 : }T = \frac{X}{\sqrt{Y/n}} \sim  t\left( n\right) \\  \text{ F分布 : }\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{ 条件 : }X \sim  {\chi }^{2}\left( {n}_{1}\right) ,Y \sim  {\chi }^{2}\left( {n}_{2}\right) ,\text{ 且 }X,\text{ 平相互独立 } \\  \text{ 结论 : }F = \frac{X/{n}_{1}}{Y/{n}_{2}} \sim  F\left( {{n}_{1},{n}_{2}}\right)  \end{array}\right.  \end{array}\right.$

例题 1. 设 ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},{X}_{3}$ 为来自正态总体 $N\left( {0,{\sigma }^{2}}\right)$ 的样本,则统计量 $S = \frac{{X}_{1} - {X}_{2}}{\sqrt{2}\left| {X}_{3}\right| }$ 服从的分布为 ( ).

(A) $F\left( {1,1}\right)$ (B) $F\left( {2,1}\right)$ (C) $t\left( 1\right)$ (D) $t\left( 2\right)$

【解】选 C。由已知条件知 ${X}_{i} \sim  N\left( {0,{\sigma }^{2}}\right)$ ,则 ${X}_{1} - {X}_{2} \sim  N\left( {0,2{\sigma }^{2}}\right)$ ,于是

$$
\frac{{X}_{1} - {X}_{2}}{\sqrt{2}\sigma } \sim  N\left( {0,1}\right) ,\frac{{X}_{3}}{\sigma } \sim  N\left( {0,1}\right) ,{\left( \frac{{X}_{3}}{\sigma }\right) }^{2} \sim  {\chi }^{2}\left( 1\right) ,
$$

因为 ${X}_{1} - {X}_{2}$ 与 ${X}_{3}$ 相互独立,所以根据 $t$ 分布的典型模式,有 $\frac{\frac{{X}_{1} - {X}_{2}}{\sqrt{2}\sigma }}{\sqrt{{\left( \frac{{X}_{3}}{\sigma }\right) }^{2}/1}} = \frac{{X}_{1} - {X}_{2}}{\sqrt{2}\left| {X}_{3}\right| } \sim  t\left( 1\right)$ .

 **4. 计算统计量的分布

重要结论: $\left\{  \begin{array}{lllll} \text{ 设 }\bar{X} = \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{X}_{i},{S}^{2} = \frac{1}{n - 1}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left( {X}_{i} - \bar{X}\right) }^{2},\text{ 则 } \\  \text{ ① }\bar{X} \sim  N\left( {\mu ,\frac{{\sigma }^{2}}{n}}\right) \text{ 或 }\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{\sigma /\sqrt{n}} \sim  N\left( {0,1}\right) \\  \text{ (2) }\frac{\left( {n - 1}\right) {S}^{2}}{{\sigma }^{2}} = \frac{1}{{\sigma }^{2}}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left( {X}_{i} - \bar{X}\right) }^{2} \sim  {\chi }^{2}\left( {n - 1}\right) \\  \text{ (3) }\frac{1}{{\sigma }^{2}}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left( {X}_{i} - \mu \right) }^{2} \sim  {\chi }^{2}\left( n\right) \\  \text{ (4) }\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{S\sqrt{n}}\sim {t(n-1) } \end{array}\right.$

例题 1. 设 ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\cdots ,{X}_{n}$ 是来自正态总体 $N\left( {\mu ,{\sigma }^{2}}\right)$ 的简单样本,记

$$
{S}_{1}^{2} = \frac{1}{n - 1}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left( {X}_{i} - \bar{X}\right) }^{2},{S}_{2}^{2} = \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left( {X}_{i} - \bar{X}\right) }^{2},
$$

$$
{S}_{3}^{2} = \frac{1}{n - 1}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left( {X}_{i} - \mu \right) }^{2},{S}_{4}^{2} = \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left( {X}_{i} - \mu \right) }^{2},
$$

则服从自由度为 $n - 1$ 的 $t$ 分布的统计量是 ( )

(A) $\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{{S}_{1}/\sqrt{n - 1}}$ (B) $\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{{S}_{2}/\sqrt{n - 1}}$ (C) $\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{{S}_{3}/\sqrt{n}}$ (D) $\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{{S}_{4}/\sqrt{n}}$ .

【解】选 B. 因为 $\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{\sigma /\sqrt{n}} \sim  N\left( {0,1}\right) ,\frac{n{S}_{2}^{2}}{{\sigma }^{2}} \sim  {\chi }^{2}\left( {n - 1}\right)$ ,所以 $\frac{\bar{X} - \mu }{{S}_{2}/\sqrt{n - 1}} \sim  t\left( {n - 1}\right)$
