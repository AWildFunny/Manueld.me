# 版块 13 向量代数

## A. 实用解题工具
### 第 1 组: 向量数量积与向量积

1. 数量积(点积,内积) : $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} = \left| \overrightarrow{a}\right| \left| \overrightarrow{b}\right| \cos \theta \left( {\theta \text{ 是向量间夹角 }}\right) \\  \text{ 坐标形式 : }\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \overrightarrow{a} = \left\{  {{x}_{1},{y}_{1},{z}_{1}}\right\}  ,\overrightarrow{b} = \left\{  {{x}_{2},{y}_{2},{z}_{2}}\right\}  \\  \overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} = {x}_{1}{x}_{2} + {y}_{1}{y}_{2} + {z}_{1}{z}_{2}. \end{array}\right.  \end{array}\right.$

2. 向量积(叉乘,外积): $\left\{  \begin{array}{lll} \text{ 表达式 : }\overrightarrow{c} = \overrightarrow{a} \times  \overrightarrow{b} \\  \text{ 大小 : }\left| \overrightarrow{c}\right|  = \left| \overrightarrow{a}\right| \left| \overrightarrow{b}\right| \sin \theta \text{ (}\theta \text{ 是向量间夹角) } \\  \text{ 公式 : }\overrightarrow{a} \times  \overrightarrow{b} = \left| \begin{array}{lll} \overrightarrow{i} & \overrightarrow{j} & \overrightarrow{k} \\  {x}_{1} & {y}_{1} & {z}_{1} \\  {x}_{2} & {y}_{2} & {z}_{2} \end{array}\right|  = \left| \begin{array}{ll} {y}_{1} & {z}_{1} \\  {y}_{2} & {z}_{2} \end{array}\right| \overrightarrow{i} - \left| \begin{array}{ll} {x}_{1} & {z}_{1} \\  {x}_{2} & {z}_{2} \end{array}\right| \overrightarrow{j} + \left| \begin{array}{ll} {x}_{1} & {y}_{1} \\  {x}_{2} & {y}_{2} \end{array}\right| \overrightarrow{k}. \end{array}\right.$

### 第 2 组: 向量混合积

1. 混合积: $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{ 定义 : }\left( {\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}}\right)  = \left( {\overrightarrow{a} \times  \overrightarrow{b}}\right)  \cdot  \overrightarrow{c}\left( {\text{ 其中 }\overrightarrow{a} = \left\{  {{x}_{1},{y}_{1},{z}_{1}}\right\}  ,\overrightarrow{b} = \left\{  {{x}_{2},{y}_{2},{z}_{2}}\right\}  ,\overrightarrow{c} = \left\{  {{x}_{3},{y}_{3},{z}_{3}}\right\}  }\right) \\  \text{ 公式 : }\left( {\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}}\right)  = \left| \begin{array}{lll} {x}_{1} & {y}_{1} & {z}_{1} \\  {x}_{2} & {y}_{2} & {z}_{2} \\  {x}_{3} & {y}_{3} & {z}_{3} \end{array}\right|  \end{array}\right.$

2. 混合积的几何意义: $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{以}\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}\text{为棱的平行六面体体积} = \left| \left( {\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}}\right) \right| \\  \overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}\text{三向量共面,则混合积}\left( {\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}}\right)  = 0 \end{array}\right.$

### 第 3 组: 向量的运算规律

1. 数量积: $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{ 交换 : }\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} = \overrightarrow{b} \cdot  \overrightarrow{a} \\  \text{ 分配 : }\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \left( {\overrightarrow{b} + \overrightarrow{c}}\right)  = \overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} + \overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{c} \end{array}\right.$

2. 向量积: $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{ 交换: }\overrightarrow{a} \times  \overrightarrow{b} =  - \left( {\overrightarrow{b} \times  \overrightarrow{a}}\right) \\  \text{ 分配: }\overrightarrow{a} \times  \left( {\overrightarrow{b} + \overrightarrow{c}}\right)  = \overrightarrow{a} \times  \overrightarrow{b} + \overrightarrow{a} \times  \overrightarrow{c} \end{array}\right.$

3. 混合积: $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{ 次序不变: }\left( {\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}}\right)  = \left( {\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c},\overrightarrow{a}}\right)  = \left( {\overrightarrow{c},\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b}}\right) \\  \text{ 次序改变: }\left( {\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}}\right)  =  - \left( {\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{c},\overrightarrow{b}}\right)  \end{array}\right.$

### 第 4 组: 空间单位向量

单位向量: $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \overrightarrow{a} = \{ x,y,z\} \text{ 对应的单位向量为 }\{ \cos \alpha ,\cos \beta ,\cos \gamma \} \\  \cos \alpha  = \frac{x}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}},\cos \beta  = \frac{y}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}},\cos \gamma  = \frac{z}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}}. \end{array}\right.$

性质: ${\cos }^{2}\alpha  + {\cos }^{2}\beta  + {\cos }^{2}\gamma  = 1$
