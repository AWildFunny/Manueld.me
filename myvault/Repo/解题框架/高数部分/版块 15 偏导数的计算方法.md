## B . 核心问题及解题方法总结

 **1. 求解多元极限** 
1. 一些简单的极限可通过一元极限的方法求解。如$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac{{e}^{xy}-1}{\sin(xy)}=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac{xy}{xy}=1$
2. 极限<span style="background:#b1ffff">存在</span>: 要求各个路径极限一致
 记住这个经典例子：
$0\leq\left|\frac{xy}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\right|\leq\left|\frac{xy}{\sqrt{2xy}}\right|\Rightarrow\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac{xy}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}=0$
 运用夹逼定理证明了$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\left|\frac{xy}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\right|=0$。
 进而推出$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac{xy}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}=0$
3. 极限<span style="background:#b1ffff">不存在</span>: 两个路径极限不同即可 (通常取特例$y=kx)$
 示例: 令$y=kx$,极限$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac{xy}{x^2+y^2}=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac k{1+k^2}$不存在
 从上面结果不难看出: <u>多元函数极限中, 同阶无穷小的极限不存在。</u>注意, 这个结论在大题中不能直接用。 
4. 连续: 极限值等于函数值 (与一元函数一致):$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(x_0,y_0)}f(x,y)=f(x_0,y_0)$
 **2. 定义法求解多元函数偏导**
$f'_x(x_0,y_0)=\lim\limits_{\Delta x\to0}\dfrac{f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0)-f(x_0,y_0)}{\Delta x};\quad f'_y(x_0,y_0)=\lim\limits_{\Delta y\to0}\dfrac{f(x_0,y_0+\Delta y)-f(x_0,y_0)}{\Delta y}$
 【笔记】求偏导数$f_x^{\prime}(x_0,y_0)$时,$y$值不变, 保持为$y_0$.仅有$x$值改变, 从$x_0$增加到$x_0+\Delta x$。此时$f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0)-f(x_0,y_0)$得到的是关于$x$的偏增量。
 例题 1. 设$f(x,y)=e^{\sqrt{x^2+y^4}}-1$,则$f(x,y)$在 (0,0)处 ( ).
 (A)对$x$可偏导, 对$y$不可偏导
 (B)对$x$不可偏导, 对$y$可偏导 
 (C)对$x$可偏导, 对$y$可偏导
 (D)对$x$不可偏导, 对$y$也不可偏导
 【解】因为$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x\to0}\frac{f(x,0)-f(0,0)}x=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x\to0}\frac{e^{|x|}}x=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x\to0}\frac{|x|}x$
 不存在, 所以$f(x,y)$在 (0,0)处对$x$不可偏导; 因为$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{y\to0}\frac{f(0,y)-f(0,0)}y=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{y\to0}\frac{e^{y^2}}y=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{y\to0}\frac{y^2}y=0$,所以$f_y^{\prime}(0,0)=0,$即$f(x,y)$在$(0,0)$处对$y$可偏导，应选（B）.
 **3. 证明多元函数可微**

1. 可微判别式:$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{\rho\to0}\frac{\Delta z-(f_x^{\prime}\Delta x+f_y^{\prime}\Delta y)}\rho=0$
其中$\rho=\sqrt{\left(\Delta x\right)^{2}+\left(\Delta y\right)^{2}};\Delta z=f(x_{0}+\Delta x,y_{0}+\Delta y)-f(x_{0},y_{0})$
另一种写法:$\Delta z=A\Delta x+B\Delta y+o(\sqrt{\left(\Delta x\right)^2+\left(\Delta y\right)^2})$
2. 结论$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x\to0,y\to0}\frac{f(x,y)-f(0,0)}{\left(x^2+y^2\right)^k}$存在, 当$k>\frac{1}{2}$时,$f(x,y)$在原点处可微
 结论证明如下:
$$
\lim\limits_{\overset{x\to0}{y\to0}}\frac{f(x,y)-f(0,0)}{\left(x^2+y^2\right)^k}\text{存在}\Rightarrow\lim\limits_{\overset{x\to0}{y\to0}}\frac{f(x,y)-f(0,0)}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\cdot\frac{1}{\left(x^2+y^2\right)^{k-\frac{1}{2}}}\text{存在}
$$
$k>\frac{1}{2}\text{时},\frac{1}{\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{k-\frac{1}{2}}}\rightarrow\infty,\text{此时必有}\frac{f(x,y)-f(0,0)}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}}\rightarrow0,\text{即}\mathop {\lim }\limits_{\underset{y\rightarrow0}{x\rightarrow0}}\frac{f(x,y)-f(0,0)}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}}=0$
从而$f(\Delta x,\Delta y)-f(0,0)=0\Delta x+0\Delta y+o(\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2})$,由定义可知,$f(x,y)$在 (0,0)处可微
 **4. 判别多元函数偏导是否连续**
偏导数连续
$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(x_{0},y_{0})}f_{x}^{\prime}(x,y)=f_{x}^{\prime}(x_{0},y_{0});\mathop {\lim }\limits_{(x,y)\to(x_{0},y_{0})}f_{y}^{\prime}(x,y)=f_{y}^{\prime}(x_{0},y_{0})$
【笔记】$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x\to x_0}f_x^{\prime}(x,y_0)=f_x^{\prime}(x_0,y_0)$不能称为偏导数连续。
 **5. 利用链式法则求极限**
链式法则: 设$z=f(u,v),u=\phi(x,v),v=\phi(x,v),$则$\begin{cases}\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\cdot\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\cdot\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\cdot\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\cdot\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\end{cases}$
 **6. 利用隐函数存在定理求偏导**
 若$F(x,y,z)$在点$(x_0,y_0,z_0)$的某一邻域内有连续的偏导数, 
 且$F(x_0,y_0,z_0)=0$,$F^{\prime}_z(x_0,y_0,z_0)\neq0$
 则有如下两条结论:
1. 在点$(x_0,y_0,z_0)$的某一邻域内能唯一确定偏导数连续的$z=f(x,y)$
2.$\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial x}=-\dfrac{F’_x(x,y,z)}{F’_z(x,y,z)},\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial y}=-\dfrac{F’_y(x,y,z)}{F’_z(x,y,z)}$
 【笔记】若$F_x^{\prime}(x_0,y_0,z_0)\neq0,F_y^{\prime}(x_0,y_0,z_0)\neq0$,则$x,y$也可分别做函数
 **7. 利用全微分求偏导**
1. 全微分:$dz=Adx+Bdy\Rightarrow A=\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial x},B=\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial y}$
 【笔记】(1). 全微分可一次性求出所有偏导数
 (2).$\quad Adx+Bdy$不一定都能写成$Adx+Bdy=dz$,仅有$\frac{\partial A}{\partial y}=\frac{\partial B}{\partial x}$时, 才能写成全微分$Adx+Bdy=dz$。这在曲线积分与路径无关及微分方程中非常重要。
2. 全微分形式不变性：$z=f\left(u,v\right),u=u\left(x,y\right),v=v\left(x,y\right)\Rightarrow dz=\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy,dz=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}du+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}dv$
 **8. 求解退化的多元函数的偏导**
 方法: 多元函数$f(x,y)$,退化成为$f(x,y(x))$,对方程$f(x,y(x))=g(x)$两边同时求导, 可求解相关试题。
例题 1. 设数$f\left(x,y\right)$满足方程$\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial x^2}=\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial y^2}$及条件$f\left(x,2x\right)=x,f_x^{\prime}\left(x,2x\right)=x^2,$则$f_{xx}^{\prime\prime}\left(x,2x\right)$为 (   )
$({A})\:\frac{5x}{3}\quad({B})\:\frac{4x}{3}\quad({C})\:-\frac{4x}{3}\quad({D})\:-\frac{5x}{3}$
 【解】选 C。由$f\left(x,2x\right)=x$两边对$x$求导, 得$f_x^{\prime}(x,2x)+2f_y^{\prime}(x,2x)=1$
$\begin{aligned}&\text{再由}f_{x}^{\prime}\big (x, 2 x\big)=x^{2},\text{得}f_{y}^{\prime}\big (x, 2 x\big)=\frac{1}{2}\big (1-x^{2}\big). \\&\text{再把}f_{x}^{\prime}\big (x, 2 x\big)=x^{2}\text{及}f_{y}^{\prime}\big (x, 2 x\big)=\frac{1}{2}\big (1-x^{2}\big)\text{两边同时对}X\text{求偏导得} \\&\begin{cases}{f_{xx}}^{\prime\prime}\left (x, 2 x\right)+2{f_{xy}}^{\prime\prime}\left (x, 2 x\right)=2 x,\\{f_{yx}}^{\prime\prime}\left (x, 2 x\right)+2{f_{yy}}^{\prime\prime}\left (x, 2 x\right)=-x,\end{cases} \\&\text{利用题设 }f_{xx}^{\prime\prime}=f_{yy}^{\prime\prime},\text{ 可解得 }f_{x}^{\prime\prime}\left (x, 2 x\right)=-\frac 43 x.\text{ 故选 C}.\end{aligned}$
 **9. 求解二阶偏导**
 偏导的链式法则: 若$z=f\left[u(x,y),v(x,y)\right]$,则$f_x^{\prime},f_y^{\prime},f_{xx}^{\prime\prime},f_{xy}^{\prime\prime},f_{yy}^{\prime\prime}$与$f$具有相同复合关系

 **10. 按$x$分段的多元函数**
方法:$\int_0^1\Bigl|xy-t\Bigr|dt=\begin{cases}\int_0^{xy}(xy-t)dt+\int_{xy}^1(t-xy)dt,0<xy<1\\\\\int_0^1(xy-t)dt\quad,&xy>1\\\\\int_0^1(t-xy)dt\quad,&xy<0\end{cases}$
例题 1. 设$z=\int_0^1|xy-t|f(t)dt,0\leq t\leq1,0\leq x,y\leq1$,其中$f(x)$为连续函数, 则
$z_{xx}^{\prime\prime}+z_{yy}^{\prime\prime}=$
【解】
$\begin{aligned}z&=\int_0^1\Bigl|xy-t\Bigr|f\Bigl(t\Bigr)dt=\int_0^{xy}\Bigl(xy-t\Bigr)f\Bigl(t\Bigr)dt+\int_{xy}^1\Bigl(t-xy\Bigr)f\Bigl(t\Bigr)dt\\[1ex]&=xy\int_0^{xy}f\Bigl(t\Bigr)dt-\int_0^{xy}tf\Bigl(t\Bigr)dt+\int_{xy}^1tf\bigl(t\Bigr)dt-xy\int_{xy}^1f\bigl(t\bigr)dt\end{aligned}$
$\begin{aligned}z_{x}^{\prime}& =y\int_{0}^{xy}f\left (t\right)+xy^{2}f\left (xy\right)-xy^{2}f\left (xy\right)-xy^{2}f\left (xy\right)+xy^{2}f\left (xy\right)-y\int_{xy}^{1}f\left (t\right) dt  \\&=y\int_{0}^{xy}f\left (t\right) dt-y\int_{xy}^{1}f\left (t\right) dt\end{aligned}$
$z_{xx}^{\prime\prime}=y^{2}f\left(xy\right)+y^{2}f\left(xy\right)=2y^{2}f\left(xy\right)$
由对称性知,$z_{yy}^{\prime\prime}=2x^{2}f\left(xy\right)$,
则$z_{xx}^{\prime\prime}+z_{yy}^{\prime\prime}=2\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)f\left(xy\right).$
