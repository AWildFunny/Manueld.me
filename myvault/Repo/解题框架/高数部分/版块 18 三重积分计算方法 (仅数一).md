# 版块 18 三重积分计算方法 (仅数一)

## A. 实用解题工具

### 第 1 组: 三重积分解题框架

## B. 核心问题及解题方法总结

 **1.三重积分简化

1. 若积分区域关于 ${yOz}$ 坐标面对称,被积函数具有奇偶性,则

$$
{\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) {dV} = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} 0,f\text{ 是 }x\text{ 的奇函数 } \\  {\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) {dV},f\text{ 是 }x\text{ 的偶函数,}{\Omega }_{1}\text{ 是 }\Omega \text{ 前半部分 } \end{array}\right.
$$

2. 同理。 $f$ 是 $y$ 的奇偶函数或 $f$ 是 $z$ 的奇偶函数时,也有类似的性质。

3. 轮换对称 $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \text{ 若积分区域关于 }x,y\text{ 轮换对称 } \\  \text{ 则 }{\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) \mathrm{d}V = {\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {y,x,z}\right) \mathrm{d}V \end{array}\right.$

【笔记】轮换对称对函数 $f\left( {x,y,z}\right)$ 无要求

 **2.三重积分直角坐标

直角坐标 $\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} {\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) \mathrm{d}V = {\int }_{a}^{b}{dx}{\int }_{{y}_{1}\left( x\right) }^{{y}_{2}\left( y\right) }{dy}{\int }_{{z}_{1}\left( {x,y}\right) }^{{z}_{2}\left( {x,y}\right) }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) {dz} \\  \text{ 其他积分展开次序同理 } \end{array}\right.$ :

 **3.三重积分柱坐标

1. 公式: ${\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) \mathrm{d}V = {\int }_{{\theta }_{1}}^{{\theta }_{2}}{d\theta }{\int }_{{r}_{1}\left( \theta \right) }^{{r}_{2}\left( \theta \right) }{rdr}{\int }_{{z}_{1}\left( {x,y}\right) }^{{z}_{2}\left( {x,y}\right) }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) {dz}$

2. 适用情况：投影区域边界含圆或圆弧

3. 柱坐标与直角坐标的关系为 $\left\{  \begin{matrix} x = r\cos \theta , & 0 \leq  r <  + \infty , \\  y = r\sin \theta , & 0 \leq  \theta  \leq  {2\pi }, \\  z = z, &  - \infty  < z <  + \infty . \end{matrix}\right.$

4. 体积微元： ${dv} = {rdrd\theta dz}$
$$
{\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) {dv} = {\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {r\cos \theta ,r\sin \theta ,z}\right) {rdrd\theta dz}
$$

例题 1. 计算 ${\iiint }_{\Omega }z{e}^{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}{dV}$ ,其中 $\Omega$ 是由曲面 $z = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}$ 以及平面 $z = 2$ 所围成的区域.

【解】 ${\iiint }_{\Omega }z{e}^{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}{dV} = {\iiint }_{\Omega }z{e}^{{r}^{2}}{rdrd\theta dz} = {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{d\theta }{\int }_{0}^{2}{e}^{{r}^{2}}{rdr}{\int }_{r}^{2}{zdz}$

$$
= {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }\left\lbrack  {{\int }_{0}^{2}{e}^{{r}^{2}} \cdot  \frac{1}{2}\left( {{2}^{2} - {r}^{2}}\right) {rdr}}\right\rbrack  {d\theta } = \pi {\int }_{0}^{2}{e}^{{r}^{2}}\left( {{2}^{2} - {r}^{2}}\right) {rdr} = \frac{\pi }{2}\left( {{e}^{4} - 5}\right) .
$$

 **4.三重积分球坐标

1. 球坐标 $\begin{cases} \text{ 公式: } & {\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) \mathrm{d}V \\   &  = {\int }_{{\varphi }_{1}}^{{\varphi }_{2}}{d\varphi }{\int }_{{\theta }_{1}}^{{\theta }_{2}}{d\theta }{\int }_{{r}_{1}}^{{r}_{2}}f\left( {r\sin \varphi \cos \theta ,r\sin \varphi \sin \theta ,r\cos \varphi }\right) {r}^{2}\sin {\varphi dr} \end{cases}$

2. 适用情况: 积分区域含球或锥

3. 球坐标与直角坐标的关系为 $\left\{  \begin{matrix} x = r\sin \varphi \cos \theta , & 0 \leq  r <  + \infty , \\  y = r\sin \varphi \sin \theta , & 0 \leq  \varphi  \leq  \pi , \\  z = r\cos \varphi , & 0 \leq  \theta  \leq  {2\pi }. \end{matrix}\right.$

4. 体积微元： ${dV} = {r}^{2}\sin {\varphi drd\varphi d\theta }$
$$
{\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) {dV} = {\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {r\sin \varphi \cos \theta ,r\sin \varphi \sin \theta ,r\cos \varphi }\right) {r}^{2}\sin {\varphi drd\theta dz}
$$

例题 1. 设 $f\left( x\right)$ 在 $x = 0$ 处可导, $f\left( 0\right)  = 0$ ,求极限 $\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}V}{\left( {1 - \cos {t}^{2}}\right) \arcsin t}$ ,其中

$\Omega  : \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}} \leq  z \leq  \sqrt{{t}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}.$

【解】运用球坐标化简三重积分

$$
{\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}V = {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }\mathrm{d}\theta {\int }_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{4}}\sin \varphi \mathrm{d}\varphi {\int }_{0}^{t}f\left( {r}^{2}\right) {r}^{2}\mathrm{\;d}r = {2\pi }\left( {1 - \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}\right) {\int }_{0}^{t}f\left( {r}^{2}\right) {r}^{2}\mathrm{\;d}r
$$

$$
\left( {1 - \cos {t}^{2}}\right) \arcsin t \sim  \frac{1}{2}{t}^{5}\left( {t \rightarrow  0}\right)
$$

则原式 $= 2\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  0}}\frac{1}{{t}^{5}}{\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}\right) {dv} = {4\pi }\left( {1 - \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}\right) \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{\int }_{0}^{t}f\left( {r}^{2}\right) {r}^{2}{dr}}{{t}^{5}} = {4\pi }\left( {1 - \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}\right) \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{t}^{2}f\left( {t}^{2}\right) }{5{t}^{4}}$

$$
= \frac{4\pi }{5}\left( {1 - \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}\right) \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  0}}\frac{f\left( {t}^{2}\right)  - f\left( 0\right) }{{t}^{2}} = \frac{4\pi }{5}\left( {1 - \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}\right) {f}^{\prime }\left( 0\right)
$$

 【说明】本题用到球坐标,变换关系及参数范围是: $\left\{  {\begin{array}{lll} x = r\cos \theta \sin \varphi \\  y = r\sin \theta \sin \varphi \\  z = r\cos \varphi  \end{array}\left( {0 \leq  \theta  \leq  {2\pi },0 \leq  \varphi  \leq  \frac{\pi }{4},0 \leq  r \leq  t}\right) }\right.$

 **5.三重积分先二后一

先二后一公式: ${\iiint }_{\Omega }f\left( {x,y,z}\right) \mathrm{d}V = {\int }_{{z}_{1}}^{{z}_{2}}\left\lbrack  {{\iint }_{{D}_{z}}f\left( {x,y,z}\right) {d\sigma }}\right\rbrack  {dz}$

适用情况: 被积函数仅与 $z$ 有关,横截面面积容易求出

【笔记】也可垂直于 $x$ 或 $y$ 轴作截面进行先二后一积分。

例题 1. 计算三重积分 $I = {\iiint }_{\Omega }\left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}\right) {dv}$ ,其中 $\Omega$ 是由 ${yOz}$ 平面内 $z = 0,z = 2$ 以及曲线  ${y}^{2} - {\left( z - 1\right) }^{2} = 1$ 所围成的平面区域绕 $z$ 轴旋转而成的空间区域.

【解】: 由题设知,区域 $\Omega$ 是由旋转面 ${x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - {\left( z - 1\right) }^{2} = 1$ 与平面 $z = 0,z = 2$ 所围成. 用与 $z$ 轴垂直的

平面截立体 $\Omega$ ,设截面为 ${D}_{z}$ ,于是 $\;I = {\int }_{0}^{2}{dz}{\iint }_{{D}_{z}}\left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}\right) {dxdy}$ .

显然 ${D}_{z}$ 是圆域,圆心为 $\left( {0,0,z}\right) \left( {0 \leq  z \leq  2}\right)$ ,半径为 $r = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}} = \sqrt{1 + {\left( z - 1\right) }^{2}}$ ,

所以 $I = {\int }_{0}^{2}{dz}{\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{d\theta }{\int }_{0}^{\sqrt{1 + {\left( z - 1\right) }^{2}}}{r}^{3}{dr} = {2\pi }{\int }_{0}^{2}\frac{1}{4}{\left\lbrack  1 + {\left( z - 1\right) }^{2}\right\rbrack  }^{2}{dz}$

$$
= \frac{\pi }{2}{\int }_{0}^{2}\left\lbrack  {1 + 2{\left( z - 1\right) }^{2} + {\left( z - 1\right) }^{4}}\right\rbrack  {dz} = \frac{28\pi }{15}\text{.}
$$
