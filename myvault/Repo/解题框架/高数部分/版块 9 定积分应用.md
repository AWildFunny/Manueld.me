## A.实用解题工具
### **第 1 组：特殊曲线**
**心形线**：
表达式：
极坐标：$r=a(1+\cos\theta),\theta\in[0,2\pi],a>0$
直角坐标：$x^2+y^2-ax=a\sqrt{x^2+y^2},a>0$
参数方程：$\left.\left\{\begin{matrix}x=a(1+cos\theta)cos\theta\\y=a(1+cos\theta)sin\theta\end{matrix}\right.\right.(a>0,\theta\in[0,2\pi])$

图形：
![[心形线.png]]

弧长$L$：
$\begin{aligned}&L=2\int_{0}^{\pi}\sqrt{\rho^{2}(\theta)+[\rho^{\prime}(\theta)]^{2}}d\theta  \\&=2\int_{0}^{\pi}\sqrt{a^{2}(1+cos\theta)^{2}+a^{2}sin^{2}\theta}\cdot d\theta  \\&=2 a\int_0^\pi\sqrt{(1+cos\theta)^2+sin^2\theta}\cdot d\theta (a>0) \\&=2\sqrt{2}a\int_{0}^{\pi}\sqrt{1+cos\theta}\cdot d\theta  \\&=4 a\int_0^\pi\left|cos\frac\theta 2\right|d\theta=8 a\end{aligned}$
面积$A$:
$\begin{aligned}&A=2\int_{0}^{\pi}\frac{1}{2}[\rho (\theta)]^{2}d\theta=\int_{0}^{\pi}a^{2}(1+cos\theta)^{2}d\theta  \\&=a^2\int_0^\pi (1+cos^2\theta+2 cos\theta) d\theta  \\&=a^2 (\pi+2\cdot\frac 12\cdot\frac\pi 2)=\frac{3\pi}2 a^2\end{aligned}$
体积$V$:
$\begin{aligned}&D=\{(r,\theta)|r=a (1-cos\theta),\theta\in[0,\pi]\} \\&r (x, y)=f (x)=y \\&V=2\pi\iint_{D}r (x, y)\mathrm{d}\sigma  \\&=2\pi\iint_{D}y\mathrm{d}xdy \\&=2\pi\int_{0}^{\pi}d\theta\int_{0}^{r (\theta)}rsin\theta\cdot rdr \\&=2\pi\int_0^\pi sin\theta d\theta\int_0^{r (\theta)}r^2 dr \\&=2\pi\int_{0}^{\pi}sin\theta\cdot\frac{1}{3}r^{3}|_{0}^{r (\theta)}d\theta  \\&=\frac{2\pi}3\int_{0}^{\pi}r^{3}\left (\theta\right) sin\theta d\theta  \\&=\frac{8\pi}{3}a^{3}\end{aligned}$
表面积$S$:
$\begin{aligned}&S=2\pi\int_\alpha^\beta|r (\theta) sin\theta|\sqrt{[r (\theta)]^2+[r^{\prime}(\theta)]^2}d\theta  \\&=2\pi\int_0^\pi|a (1-cos\theta) sin\theta|\sqrt{[a (1-cos\theta)]^2+[asin\theta]^2}d\theta  \\&=2\pi a^2\int_0^\pi (1-cos\theta) sin\theta\sqrt{[(1-cos\theta)]^2+[sin\theta]^2}d\theta  \\&=2\sqrt{2}\pi a^2\int_0^\pi (1-cos\theta) sin\theta\sqrt{1-cos\theta}d\theta  \\&=2\sqrt{2}\pi a^2\int_{\pi}^0 (1-cos\theta)\sqrt{1-cos\theta}d (cos\theta) \\&=2\sqrt 2\pi a^2\int_{-1}^1 (1-u)^{\frac 32}du \\&=\frac{32\pi}5 a^{2}\end{aligned}$

极坐标：${r=a\theta}$，$\theta\in[0,+\infty),a>0$
参数方程：$\left\{\begin{matrix}x=a\theta\cdot cos\theta\\y=a\theta\cdot sin\theta\end{matrix}\right.(\theta\in[0,+\infty),a>0)$
图形：
**注：这里只展示θ在[0,2π]上的图像。**
![[阿基米德螺线.png]]

弧长$L$：
$\begin{aligned}&L=\int_0^{2\pi}\sqrt{[r (\theta)]^2+[r^{\prime}(\theta)]^2}d\theta  \\&=\int_{0}^{2\pi}\sqrt{a^{2}\theta^{2}+a^{2}}d\theta  \\&=a^2\int_0^{2\pi}\sqrt{\theta^2+1}d\theta  \\&=\frac a 2[2\pi\sqrt{1+4\pi^{2}}+ln (2\pi+\sqrt{1+4\pi^{2}})]\end{aligned}$
不定积分公式：
$\begin{aligned}&\int\sqrt{x^{2}+a^{2}}dx \\&=\frac x 2\sqrt{x^2+a^2}+\frac{a^2}2 ln (x+\sqrt{x^2+a^2})+C\end{aligned}$
面积$A$：
$\begin{aligned}&A=\int_{0}^{2\pi}\frac{1}{2}[r (\theta)]^{2}d\theta=\frac{a^{2}}{2}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\theta^{2}d\theta  \\&=\frac{4\pi^{3}}{3}a^{2}\end{aligned}$

**笛卡尔叶形线：**
表达式：
直角坐标：$x^3+y^3=3axy,a>0$
极坐标：
$r=\dfrac{3asin\theta cos\theta}{sin^3\theta+cos^3\theta},\theta\in[0,\frac{3\pi}{4})\cup(\frac{3\pi}{4},\pi],a>0$
参数方程：$\left.\left\{\begin{matrix}x=\frac{3at}{1+t^3}\\y=\frac{3at^2}{1+t^3}\end{matrix}\right.\right.(a>0,t\neq-1)$
图形：
![[笛卡尔叶形线.png]]
渐近线：
当 t→-1时，x→∞，y→∞，故叶形线没有水平与铅直渐近线

$\begin{aligned}&\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to-1}{\frac{y}{x}}=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to-1}t=-1=k \\&\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to-1}\left (y-kx\right) \\&=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to-1}(\frac{3 at^{2}}{1+t^{3}}+\frac{3 at}{1+t^{3}}) \\&=\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to-1}\frac{3 at (t+1)}{1+t^3}=-a\end{aligned}$
故叶形线有唯一的斜渐近线$y=-x-a$
***笔记→参数方程求渐近线：***
对于曲线的参数方程
$\left.\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=x(t)\\y=y(t)\end{array}\right.\right.$
**①若**$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_{0}}x(t)=\infty,\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_{0}}y(t)=b,$
则$y=b$为水平渐近线。
**②若**$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_0}x(t)=c,\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_0}y(t)=\infty,$
**③若**$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_0}x(t)=\infty,\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_0}y(t)=\infty,$
$\begin{aligned}&\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_0}\frac{y(t)}{x(t)}=k\neq0,\\\\&\mathop {\lim }\limits_{t\to t_0}\left[y(t)-kx(t)\right]=b,\end{aligned}$
**则$y=kx+b$为斜渐近线。**
面积$A$：
记第一象限围成的面积为$A$
$\begin{aligned}&A=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\frac{1}{2}[r (\theta)]^{2}d\theta=\frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(\frac{3 asin\theta cos\theta}{sin^{3}\theta+cos^{3}\theta})^{2}d\theta  \\&=\frac{9 a^2}2\int_0^{\frac\pi 2}\frac{sin^2\theta cos^2\theta}{(sin^3\theta+cos^3\theta)^2}d\theta  \\&=\frac{9 a^2}2\int_0^{\frac\pi 2}\frac{sin^2\theta cos^2\theta\cdot sec^6\theta}{(sin^3\theta+cos^3\theta)^2\cdot sec^6\theta}d\theta  \\&=\frac{9 a^2}2\int_0^{\frac\pi 2}\frac{tan^2\theta\cdot sec^2\theta}{(tan^3\theta+1)^2}d\theta  \\&=\frac{9 a^2}2\int_0^{\frac\pi 2}\frac{tan^2\theta}{(tan^3\theta+1)^2}d (tan\theta) \\&=\frac{9 a^2}2\int_{0}^{+\infty}\frac{u^2}{(u^3+1)^2}du \\&=\frac{3 a^{2}}{2}\int_{0}^{+\infty}\frac{d (u^{3}+1)}{(u^{3}+1)^{2}} \\&=\left.\frac{3 a^{2}}{2}\cdot\frac{1}{u^{3}+1}\right|_{u=+\infty}^{u=0}\end{aligned}$
$=\dfrac{3a^2}2$

**对数螺旋线：**
表达式： 
极坐标：$r=ae^{b\theta}$
参数方程：$\begin{cases}x=a\cdot e^{b\theta}\cos\theta\\y=a\cdot e^{b\theta}\sin\theta\end{cases}$
图形：
![[对数螺旋线.jpg]]
弧长$L$:
$\mathrm{d}S=\sqrt{\left(e^\theta\right)^2+\left[\frac{\mathrm{d}\left(e^\theta\right)}{\mathrm{d}\theta}\right]^2}\mathrm{d}\theta=\sqrt{e^{2\theta}+e^{2\theta}}\mathrm{d}\theta=\sqrt{2e^{2\theta}}\mathrm{d}\theta=\sqrt{2}e^{\theta}\mathrm{d}\theta;$
$\int_{0}^{\pi}\mathrm{d}S=\int_{0}^{\pi}\sqrt{2}e^{\theta}\mathrm{d}\theta=\sqrt{2}\int_{0}^{\pi}e^{\theta}\mathrm{d}\theta=\sqrt{2}\left.e^{\theta}\right|_{0}^{\pi}=\sqrt{2}\left(e^{\pi}-1\right).$
注：极坐标弧长公式的由来
$\left.\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=r\cos\theta\\y=r\sin\theta\end{array}\right.\right.;\quad\left\{\begin{array}{l}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}\theta}=\frac{\mathrm{d}r}{\mathrm{d}\theta}\cos\theta-r\sin\theta=r'\cos\theta-r\sin\theta\\\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}\theta}=\frac{\mathrm{d}r}{\mathrm{d}\theta}\sin\theta+r\cos\theta=r'\sin\theta+r\cos\theta\end{array}\right.;$

$\mathrm{d}S=\sqrt{\left(\mathrm{d}x\right)^2+\left(\mathrm{d}y\right)^2}=\sqrt{\left[\left(\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}\theta}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}\theta}\right)^2\right]\left(\mathrm{d}\theta\right)^2}$$\begin{aligned}&\begin{aligned}=\sqrt{(r'\cos\theta-r\sin\theta)^2+(r'\sin+r\cos\theta)^2}\mathrm{d}\theta\end{aligned} \\&=\sqrt{\left (r^{\prime}\right)^2\cos^2\theta-2 r^{\prime}r\cos\theta\sin\theta+r^2\sin^2\theta+\left (r^{\prime}\right)^2\sin^2\theta+2 r^{\prime}r\cos\theta\sin\theta+r^2\cos^2\theta}\operatorname{d}\theta  \\&=\sqrt{r^2\left (\sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta\right)+\left (r^{\prime}\right)^2\left (\sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta\right)}\mathrm{d}\theta  \\&=\sqrt{r^2+\left (r^{\prime}\right)^2}\mathrm{d}\theta.\end{aligned}$

表达式：
直角坐标：$\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{2}=a^{2}(x^{2}-y^{2})$$a>0$
极坐标：$r^{2}=a^{2}\cos2\theta,$$\theta\in[-\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{\pi}{4}]\cup[\frac{3\pi}{4},\frac{5\pi}{4}],a>0$
图形：
![[双扭线.png]]
弧长$L$：
$\begin{aligned}&r^{\prime}=-\frac{a\cdot sin 2\theta}{\sqrt{cos 2\theta}} \\&L=4\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sqrt{r^{2}(\theta)+[r^{\prime}(\theta)]^{2}}d\theta  \\&=4\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sqrt{a^{2}cos 2\theta+a^{2}\cdot\frac{sin^{2}2\theta}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4 a\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sqrt{cos 2\theta+\frac{sin^{2}2\theta}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4 a\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sqrt{\frac{cos^{2}2\theta}{cos 2\theta}+\frac{sin^{2}2\theta}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4 a\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sqrt{\frac{1}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4 a\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\sqrt{sec 2\theta}d\theta  \\&=2 a\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sqrt{secu}du\end{aligned}$
$\begin{aligned}&=2 a\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}cos^{-\frac{1}{2}}udu \\&=2 a\cdot\frac{\Gamma (\frac{1}{2})\Gamma (\frac{1}{4})}{2\Gamma (\frac{3}{4})} \\&=\frac{\sqrt{\pi}\cdot\Gamma (\frac{1}{4})}{\Gamma (\frac{3}{4})}a\approx 5.244 a\end{aligned}$
其中
$\begin{aligned}&\Gamma (\frac{1}{4})=\frac{\Gamma (1.25)}{0.25}=\frac{0.9064}{0.25}\approx 3.6256 \\&\Gamma (\frac{3}{4})=\frac{\Gamma (1.75)}{0.75}=\frac{0.91906}{0.75}\approx 1.2254 \\&\sqrt{\pi}\approx 1.7725\end{aligned}$
**注1：此弧长积分并非初等积分，因此结果可以保留 gamma 函数值的形式。**
**注2：上述定积分计算中用到了一类定积分的结论。**
$\begin{aligned}\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}sin^\alpha\theta\cdot cos^\beta\theta d\theta&=\frac{\Gamma(\frac{1+\alpha}{2})\Gamma(\frac{1+\beta}{2})}{2\Gamma(1+\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2})}\\\\(\alpha,\beta>-1,\alpha\in R,\beta\in R)\end{aligned}$
特别地，当α、β为正整数时，可得出更为广泛的华里士公式结论。
**注3：计算结果中的两个 gamma 函数值用到了递推公式**

***笔记→极坐标曲线的旋转体积**

双纽线绕$x$轴旋转一周形成的图形体积为$V_x$
$\begin{aligned}&V_{x}=2\cdot\frac{2\pi}{3}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}r^{3}(\theta) sin\theta d\theta  \\&=\frac{4\pi}{3}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}a^{3}cos^{\frac{3}{2}}2\theta\cdot sin\theta d\theta  \\&=\frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}cos^{\frac{3}{2}}2\theta\cdot sin\theta d\theta  \\&=\frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3}I\end{aligned}$
$\begin{aligned}&I=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}cos^{\frac{3}{2}}2\theta\cdot sin\theta d\theta  \\&=-\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}(2 cos^2\theta-1)^{\frac{3}{2}}d (cos\theta) \\&=\int_{\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}^{1}(2 u^{2}-1)^{\frac{3}{2}}du \\&=\int_{\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}^{1}[(\sqrt{2}u)^{2}-1]^{\frac{3}{2}}du \\&=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}tan^{4}\alpha sec\alpha d\alpha (\sqrt{2}u=sec\alpha) \\&=\frac{\sqrt 2}2\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}(sec^2\alpha-1)^2 sec\alpha d\alpha  \\&=\frac{\sqrt 2}2\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}(sec^5\alpha+sec\alpha-2 sec^3\alpha) d\alpha \end{aligned}$

计算$sec^{3}\alpha$的不定积分：
$\begin{aligned}&\int sec^{3}\alpha d\alpha  \\&=\int sec^{2}\alpha\cdot sec\alpha d\alpha  \\&=\int sec\alpha d (tan\alpha)\end{aligned}$
$\begin{aligned}&=sec\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-\int tan\alpha d (sec\alpha) \\&=sec\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-\int tan^{2}\alpha\cdot sec\alpha d\alpha  \\&=sec\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-\int (sec^2\alpha-1)\cdot sec\alpha d\alpha  \\&=sec\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-\int sec^{3}\alpha d\alpha+\int sec\alpha d\alpha \end{aligned}$
移项可得：
$\begin{gathered}\int sec^{3}\alpha d\alpha=\frac{1}{2}(sec\alpha\cdot tan\alpha+\int sec\alpha d\alpha) \\=\frac{1}{2}(sec\alpha\cdot tan\alpha+ln|sec\alpha+tan\alpha|)+C \end{gathered}$
**注：建议记住$sec^{3}\alpha$的不定积分结果**
计算$sec^{5}\alpha$的不定积分：
$\begin{aligned}&\int sec^5\alpha d\alpha=\int sec^3\alpha\cdot sec^2\alpha d\alpha  \\&=\int sec^3\alpha d (tan\alpha) \\&=sec^3\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-\int tan\alpha d (sec^3\alpha) \\&=sec^3\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-3\int sec^3\alpha\cdot tan^2\alpha d\alpha  \\&=sec^3\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-3\int sec^3\alpha\cdot (sec^2\alpha-1) d\alpha  \\&=sec^{3}\alpha\cdot tan\alpha-3\int sec^{5}\alpha d\alpha+3\int sec^{3}\alpha d\alpha \end{aligned}$
移项可得：
$\begin{aligned}&\int sec^{5}\alpha d\alpha=\frac{1}{4}sec^{3}\alpha\cdot tan\alpha+\frac{3}{4}\int sec^{3}\alpha d\alpha  \\&=\frac{1}{4}sec^{3}\alpha\cdot tan\alpha+\frac{3}{8}(sec\alpha\cdot tan\alpha+ln\left|sec\alpha+tan\alpha\right|)+C\end{aligned}$
代入积分上下限，得：
$\begin{aligned}&\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}sec^{5}\alpha d\alpha=\frac{7\sqrt{2}}{8}+\frac{3}{8}ln (\sqrt{2}+1) \\&\begin{aligned}\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}sec\alpha d\alpha=ln (\sqrt{2}+1)\end{aligned} \\&\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}sec^{3}\alpha d\alpha=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}+\frac{1}{2}ln (\sqrt{2}+1) \\&\therefore I=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}[\frac{3}{8}ln (\sqrt{2}+1)-\frac{\sqrt{2}}{8}] \\&\therefore V_{x}=\frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3}I=[\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4}ln (\sqrt{2}+1)-\frac{1}{6}]\pi a^{3}\end{aligned}$
双纽线绕$y$轴旋转一周形成的图形体积为$V_y$
$\begin{aligned}&V_{y}=2\cdot{\frac{2\pi}{3}}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}r^{3}(\theta) cos\theta d\theta  \\&=\frac{4\pi}3\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}a^3 cos^{\frac 32}2\theta\cdot cos\theta d\theta  \\&=\frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}cos^{\frac{3}{2}}2\theta\cdot cos\theta d\theta  \\&=\frac{4\pi a^3}3 I\end{aligned}$
$\begin{aligned}&I=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}cos^{\frac{3}{2}}2\theta\cdot cos\theta d\theta  \\&=\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}(1-2 sin^2\theta)^{\frac 32}d (sin\theta) \\&=\int_{0}^{\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}(1-2 u^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}du \\&=\int_0^{\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}[1-(\sqrt{2}u)^2]^{\frac{3}{2}}du \\&=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}cos^{4}\alpha d\alpha (\sqrt{2}u=sin\alpha) \\&=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\cdot\frac{3}{4}\cdot\frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{\pi}{2}=\frac{3\sqrt{2}\pi}{32} \\&\therefore V_{y}=\frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3}I=\frac{\pi^{2}a^{3}}{4\sqrt{2}}\end{aligned}$

表面积$S_x;S_y$

$\begin{aligned}&S_{x}=2\cdot 2\pi\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}|r (\theta) sin\theta|\sqrt{[r (\theta)]^{2}+[r^{\prime}(\theta)]^{2}}d\theta  \\&=4\pi\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}\left|a\sqrt{cos 2\theta}\cdot sin\theta\right|\sqrt{a^2 cos 2\theta+a^2\cdot\frac{sin^22\theta}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4\pi a^2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\left|\sqrt{cos 2\theta}\cdot sin\theta\right|\sqrt{\frac{cos^22\theta}{cos 2\theta}+\frac{sin^22\theta}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4\pi a^2\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\left|\sqrt{cos 2\theta}\cdot sin\theta\right|\sqrt{\frac{1}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4\pi a^2\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}sin\theta d\theta  \\&=(4-2\sqrt 2)\pi a^2\end{aligned}$

$\begin{aligned}&S_y=2\cdot 2\pi\int_0^{\frac n 4}\left|r (\theta) cos\theta\right|\sqrt{[r (\theta)]^2+[r^{\prime}(\theta)]^2}d\theta  \\&=4\pi\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}\left|a\sqrt{cos 2\theta}\cdot cos\theta\right|\sqrt{a^2 cos 2\theta+a^2\cdot\frac{sin^22\theta}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4\pi a^{2}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\left|\sqrt{cos 2\theta}\cdot cos\theta\right|\sqrt{\frac{1}{cos 2\theta}}d\theta  \\&=4\pi a^2\int_0^{\frac\pi 4}cos\theta d\theta  \\&=2\sqrt 2\pi a^2\end{aligned}$

**星形线：**
表达式：
直角坐标：$x^{\frac{2}{3}}+y^{\frac{2}{3}}=a^{\frac{2}{3}},a>0$
极坐标：$\rho=\frac{a}{(cos^\frac{2}{3}\theta+sin^\frac{2}{3}\theta)^\frac{3}{2}},\theta\in[0,2\pi],a>0$
参数方程：
$\begin{cases}x=a\cos^3\theta\\y=a\sin^3\theta\end{cases}$$(a>0,\theta\in[0,2\pi])$
图形：
![[星形线.png]]

弧长$L$:

## B. 核心问题及解题方法总结
**1. 利用古尔丁定理求体积**
**古尔丁定理**：由平面形状绕和它的同一个平面上的轴旋转而产生的族转体的体积$V$,等于平面形状面积$S$乘以平面形状的几何中心经过的距离$d_1$的积:$V=Sd_1$

 例题 1. 设$a,b>1$,则圆域$D=\left\{(x,y)|(x-a)^2+(y-1)^2\leq1\right\}$绕直线$x=-b$旋转一周所形成的旋转体的体积是
 【解】$V=Sd_{1}=\pi\cdot1^{2}\cdot2\pi\cdot(a+b)=2\pi^{2}(a+b)$
 【细节说明】圆心就是圆域的几何中心, 其坐标为$(a,1)$.圆域$D$绕直线旋转一周时, 几何中心走过的轨迹为圆$L$,其半径为$r=a+b$, 故几何中心经过的距离$d_1=2\pi r=2\pi(a+b)$。另一方面圆域的面积$S=\pi\cdot1^2=\pi$。
 所以$V=Sd_{_1}=2\pi^2(a+b)$

**3. 求解定积分的证明题
定积分证明题的主要方法有：
1. 化为变限函数，从而利用函数单调性
2. 利用积分中值定理
3. 利用$F(x)=\int_{a}^{x}f\left(t\right)dt,F^{\prime}(x)=f(x)$

 例题 1. (1994,2)设$f(x)$在$[0,1]$上连续且递城, 证明: 当$0<\lambda<1$时,$\int_0^{\lambda}f(x)dx\geq\lambda\int_0^{1}f(x)dx$
 【解法 1】用函数单调性.
令$\varphi(\lambda)=\int_0^\lambda f(x)dx-\lambda\int_0^1f(x)dx\:,\quad\lambda\in[0,1].\quad\text{则}\:\varphi^{\prime}(\lambda)=f(\lambda)-\int_0^1f(x)dx$
 令$\varphi^{\prime}(\lambda)=0\Rightarrow f(\xi)=\int_0^1f(x)dx,\xi\in(0,1)$
 由$f(\lambda)$递减,$\lambda=\xi$为唯一驻点, 且$\varphi^{\prime}(\lambda)$在$\lambda=\xi$由正变负,$\lambda=\xi$是$\varphi(\lambda)$的极大值点也是最大值点; 由此, 最小点必为端点$\lambda=0$或$1$
 【笔记】连续函数在闭区间必能取到最大和最小值。若可导函数最小值点在区间内部, 则必为驻点, 此时与驻点唯一矛盾。从而有$\varphi(\lambda)\geq\varphi(0)=\varphi(1)=0,0<\lambda<1.$
 命题得证.
 【笔记】<u>定积分不等式证明问题, 将上限换成</u>$x$, <u>就是函数单调性的问题。</u>这是非常重要的命题方式
 【解法 2】用积分中值定理。
 为分清两中值的大小, 需要分别在$(0,\lambda),(\lambda,1)$两区间内用积分中值定理:
$\int_0^1f(x)dx=\int_0^\lambda f(x)dx+\int_\lambda^1f(x)dx\:,$

$\text{由此},\int_{0}^{\lambda}f(x)dx-\lambda\int_{0}^{1}f(x)dx=(1-\lambda)\int_{0}^{\lambda}f(x)dx-\lambda\int_{\lambda}^{1}f(x)dx$

$=(1-\lambda)\cdot\lambda f(\xi_1)-\lambda\cdot(1-\lambda)f(\xi_2)=(1-\lambda)\cdot\lambda\big[f(\xi_1)-f(\xi_2)\big],$

 其中,$0<\xi_1<\lambda<\xi_2<1$: 又因$f(x)$递减,$f(\xi_1)\geq f(\xi_2)$.上式的右端大于零, 问题得证.
 【笔记】积分中值定理是定积分或重积分中非常重要的命题方式
